Reactive Glaze Bowl

Ceramics — Wheel-Thrown

A wheel-thrown ceramic bowl finished with a reactive blue glaze. The glaze chemistry produces unpredictable pooling, breaking, and color variation during high-fire reduction — every piece comes out unique. The form is kept deliberately simple so the surface can be the focus. Fired to cone 10 in a gas kiln.

Tools

Potter's wheel, gas kiln

Materials

Stoneware clay, reactive blue glaze

Techniques

Wheel throwing, high-fire reduction, glaze chemistry
