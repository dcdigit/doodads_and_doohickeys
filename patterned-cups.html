Patterned Cups

Stackable Tumblers — Glaze Pattern Series

A set of stackable tumblers with bold, graphic glaze patterns in red, yellow, blue, and green. The cups are shaped so they nest together, allowing the glaze pattern to flow seamlessly between stacked pieces. The patterns are achieved through a wax-resist technique — wax is applied in organic shapes before dipping in colored glaze, leaving the raw stoneware exposed where the wax was. The pronounced texture of the glaze protruding off the clay surface gives each cup a tactile quality.
