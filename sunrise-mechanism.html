Sunrise Mechanism

Product Design — Mechanical Engineering

An adjustment mechanism for a desk lamp that uses a ball joint and threaded rod to allow smooth, lockable positioning. The design prioritizes one-handed operation — push to adjust, release to lock. All parts are designed for injection molding with realistic draft angles, wall thicknesses, and snap-fit tolerances.
