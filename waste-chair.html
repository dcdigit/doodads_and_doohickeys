Waste Chair

Furniture — Reclaimed Materials

A chair built entirely from discarded materials. The frame is welded scrap metal — salvaged steel tube and flat bar cut and joined into a structurally sound skeleton. The seat and back are woven from sliced inner tubes, creating a surprisingly comfortable sling with natural flex. Every material in the piece was headed for a landfill.

Tools

MIG welder, angle grinder, hand tools

Materials

Scrap steel, bicycle inner tubes

Techniques

MIG welding, material reclamation, weaving
